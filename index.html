GrillBer

GrillBer is a rental and delivery service for grills! Grill renters can put up the specs and descriptions for their grills, and users can rent them for specific amounts of time! The grill owner will drop off the grill at the designated time and location, and then pick it back up when the user is finished!

FAQ

Q: How do I pay the owner of the Grill?

A: We accept Venmo and Apple Pay.

Q: Do I have to clean the grill before I return it?

A: Yes, please clean the grill after each use, and prior to returning the grill. If the grill is not returned in same fashion it was received, there will be an additional charge.

Q: How does the grill get to me?

A: Once you select your grill, you provide the owner with a date and time, and the owner will make sure you get it on time!

How? and Why GrillBer??

GrillBer eliminates the need for purchasing your own grill! Maybe your apartment doesn't let you have one, but you want one for all those tailgates you attend! Let us do all the work, and you just bring the meat and get grillin'!
